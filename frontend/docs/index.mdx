# Welcome to A Beginner's Guide to AI

This book is designed to be a starting point for anyone new to the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just a curious mind, this guide will walk you through the fundamental concepts, history, and real-world applications of AI.

Our journey is divided into four main chapters:

1.  **[Introduction to AI](./01-introduction-to-ai.mdx):** We'll start with the basics, defining what AI is and exploring its different forms.
2.  **[History of AI](./02-history-of-ai.mdx):** Journey back in time to discover the key milestones and breakthroughs that have shaped AI.
3.  **[Applications of AI](./03-applications-of-ai.mdx):** Discover how AI is already a part of our daily lives, from our phones to our hospitals.
4.  **[Future of AI](./04-future-of-ai.mdx):** Look ahead at the exciting and challenging future of AI, including the quest for machines that can think like us.

Let's begin exploring the world of Artificial Intelligence!
